Exercise: Bubble Sort

In lecture, we saw a step-by-step breakdown of how the bubble sort algorithm sorted an array of integers:

  • 2 4 6 1 3 7 5 8

First Pass:

  • 2 4 6 1 3 7 5 8

Second Pass:

  • 2 1 4 3 6 5 7 8
  • 2 1 3 4 6 5 7 8

Third Pass:

  • 2 1 3 4 5 6 7 8
